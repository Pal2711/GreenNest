About Yaad

Yaad is more than just a marketplace — it is a movement toward transforming the way agriculture operates in India. With millions of farmers relying on outdated systems, our platform aims to simplify processes, bring market clarity, and empower every stakeholder in the agricultural ecosystem.

We serve as a bridge between traditional agricultural methods and modern technological innovations. From providing live mandi rates to connecting farmers directly with wholesale buyers and retailers, Yaad is an all-in-one platform for anyone involved in farming and food production.

Our Mission

To revolutionize agriculture by offering transparent, efficient, and digitally empowered systems to farmers, buyers, and sellers across India.

Our Vision

To create a future where every farmer has the tools, access, and knowledge to thrive in a fair and sustainable agricultural ecosystem.

Our Values

Innovation, Trust, Inclusion, Sustainability, and Community — these core values define Yaad’s purpose and drive.

Why Choose Yaad?

  • Direct Farm-to-Market Access: Eliminate middlemen and increase profits.
  • Real-Time Market Data: Stay informed with live updates on crop prices and demand trends.
  • Secure Transactions: Verified users, secure payments, and dispute resolution systems.
  • Language Support: Regional language options make the platform accessible to all.
  • Agro Insights: Weather forecasts, crop care tips, and expert blogs — all in one place.

Meet Our Team

Yaad is built by a diverse group of agri-tech professionals, software developers, market analysts, and field workers who understand the needs of rural India. With deep roots in agriculture and cutting-edge knowledge in technology, our team is committed to positive change.
